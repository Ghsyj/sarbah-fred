# 🗺️ Strategic Research Roadmap: Psychometric Integrity Architecture (PIA)
**Principal Investigator:** Sarbah Fred Junior | Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin
**Target Outcome:** Implementation of a Resilient Diagnostic Pipeline for High-Stakes Selection

## 🛡️ Epistemic Strategy
This roadmap utilizes a **Phase-Gate Risk Mitigation** structure. Each 'Gate' serves as a falsification checkpoint to ensure the structural integrity of the latent model before empirical scaling.

---

## 📅 Year 1: In-Silico Adversarial Modeling & Stress-Testing
*Goal: Defining the Asymptotic Breakdown Points of the 2PL-IRT Model.*

- **M1–M4:** **Taxonomy of Distortion:** Systematic mapping of kinetic latency ($\tau$) and response variability ($IRV$) across stochastic and deterministic faking conditions.
- **M5–M8:** **Adversarial Monte Carlo Environment:** Development of recovery protocols to establish 'Information Sensitivity' thresholds. 
    - *Metric:* Determination of the Minimum Viable Sample ($N_{MVS}$) required to maintain parameter recovery under 25% adversarial noise.
- **M9–M12:** **[GATE 1: RESILIENCE VALIDATION]** Internal audit. Submission of Manuscript 1: *"Adversarial Resilience in IRT: A Monte Carlo Evaluation of Parameter Stability."*

## 📅 Year 2: Empirical In-Vivo Implementation & XAI Bridge
*Goal: Operationalizing the XAI-Kinetic Bridge in Real-World Selection Manifolds.*

- **M13–M16:** IRB (Ethik-Kommission) approval & **Latency Hardware Calibration** (Correcting for device-driven variance).
- **M17–M20:** **Active Data Ingestion:** Deployment to high-stakes selection pools ($N \approx 1000$). Collection of behavioral telemetry.
- **M21–M24:** **[GATE 2: ALGORITHMIC ACCOUNTABILITY]** SHAP-interpreted mapping of the kinetic-static interface. 
    - *Key Task:* Decoupling 'Cognitive Friction' from 'Latent Ability' to satisfy Art. 13-15 of the EU AI Act.

## 📅 Year 3: Synthesis, Cross-Validation & Defense
*Goal: Theoretical Closure and Dissemination of the Integrity Framework.*

- **M25–M28:** **Generalizability Audit:** Testing against independent hold-out datasets. Implementation of **Second-Order Distortion Checks** (Anticipating candidate adaptation).
- **M29–M32:** Cumulative Thesis Assembly (3-Article format); Submission to the Faculty of Life Sciences.
- **M33–M36:** Dissemination of the **Open-Source Integrity Protocol**; Final Disputation and Doctoral Defense.
